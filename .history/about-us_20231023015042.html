About Stones Village

Our Vision

At Stones Village, we embrace the timeless elegance of stone. Each project, from Granite to Porcelain, is a testament to our dedication and craftsmanship. Based in Concord, Ontario, Stones Village stands out as a leading supplier and craftsman in stone fabrication, offering unparalleled artistry.

Our Legacy

Established in 2002 by Mr. Ahmad Abedi, Stones Village has been setting industry standards, continuously elevating the bar in stone craftsmanship. Our commitment is unwavering. Every project is a reflection of our expertise and passion for stone fabrication.
