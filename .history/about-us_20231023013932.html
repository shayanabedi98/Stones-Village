About Stones Village

Our Vision

At Stones Village, we believe in the timeless beauty of stone. As purveyors of Granite, Marble, Onyx, Quartz, and Porcelain, we are more than just suppliers; we are craftsmen. Every slab, every cut, and every installation tells a story of our dedication, precision, and passion.

Who We Are

Nestled in the heart of Concord, Ontario, Stones Village has become a name synonymous with quality and craftsmanship. With an unparalleled expertise in sourcing, fabricating, and installing high-quality stones, we have transformed countless spaces into masterpieces.

Our Expertise

Our team of seasoned craftsmen takes pride in every project we undertake. From the first measurement to the final piece of stone laid, we ensure meticulous attention to detail, precision, and superior quality. Our commitment extends beyond just our craft; we believe in fostering strong relationships with our clients. That's why we offer free, detailed quotations to help bring your visions closer to reality.

Our Legacy

Established by the vision and perseverance of our founder, Mr. Ahmad Abedi, Stones Village stands tall with years of experience. We have faced challenges, set benchmarks, and have continuously redefined what's possible with stone.

Our Promise

At Stones Village, we don't just work with stones; we breathe life into them. We are committed to ensuring every project we undertake is a testament to our mastery and love for stone craftsmanship. Let us transform your spaces and bring your visions to life.
