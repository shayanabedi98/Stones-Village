About Us

Welcome to Stones Village, the epitome of excellence in stone supply, precise fabrication, bespoke design, and expert installation services. Initially founded in the lively city of Dubai in 2002 by the visionary Ahmad Abedi, we have since expanded our operations and proudly established our presence in Vaughan, Ontario, Canada. Today, we are delighted to offer our exceptional services to clients across the entire country.

Our Mission

At Stones Village, our mission transcends the mere provision of services; we strive to transform your living and working spaces into captivating works of art. Utilizing the unmatched elegance and inherent beauty of stones such as Marble, Onyx, Granite, Quartz, and Porcelain, we specialize in enhancing kitchens, bathrooms, countertops, backsplashes, and vanity areas with a touch of luxury, sophistication, and timeless beauty.

Our Vision

Ahmad Abedi founded Stones Village with a clear vision—to revolutionize the standards of stone craftsmanship and set a new benchmark for quality and customer satisfaction. His passion for excellence, attention to the finest details, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction have been the guiding principles of our journey, propelling us to achieve new heights and earn a stellar reputation in the industry.

Services

Stone Supply

We take immense pride in our exquisite selection of high-quality stones, each carefully chosen to ensure it meets our rigorous standards of beauty and durability. From the elegant and timeless beauty of Marble and the unique, eye-catching patterns of Onyx to the unparalleled durability of Granite, the versatility of Quartz, and the modern appeal of Porcelain, our inventory is curated to offer you the very best.

Design

Our in-house design team is here to bring your vision to life, working collaboratively with you every step of the way. We provide expert guidance, innovative design solutions, and a deep understanding of stone aesthetics to ensure that the stone you select perfectly complements and enhances the overall look and feel of your space.

Fabrication

At the heart of our fabrication services lies the Denver Teknika Elite 5, a top-tier, precision-driven machine renowned for its reliability and accuracy. This state-of-the-art technology empowers us to cut and finish any stone to your exact specifications, ensuring a level of perfection and attention to detail that is second to none.

Installation

Our experienced and meticulous installation team is dedicated to ensuring a flawless finish for every project. We pay close attention to every detail, from the initial measurements to the final placement, ensuring that your stone features are installed with precision and care, guaranteeing longevity and beauty for years to come.
