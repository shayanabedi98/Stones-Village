About Us

Welcome to Stones Village, where unparalleled quality meets masterful craftsmanship in the realm of stone supply, fabrication, design, and installation services. Established in 2002 in Dubai by the visionary Ahmad Abedi, we have since expanded our horizons and now proudly serve the Vaughan, Ontario community in Canada.

Our Mission

At Stones Village, we are on a mission to transform spaces into breathtaking works of art, utilizing the timeless elegance and natural beauty of stones like Marble, Onyx, Granite, Quartz, and Porcelain. We specialize in elevating kitchens, bathrooms, countertops, backsplashes, and vanity areas with luxury and sophistication.

Our Vision

Ahmad Abedi laid the foundation of Stones Village with a dream to set a new standard in stone craftsmanship. His unwavering commitment to quality, meticulous attention to detail, and dedication to customer satisfaction continue to drive our success and reputation in the industry.
